Our Story of Joy and Innovation

Founded by Pushpalatha Suresh, Soft Toy Paradise has been bringing smiles to children's faces and creating opportunities for artisans since 1996.

Our Founder

Pushpalatha Suresh

Born and raised in Bangalore, Pushpalatha Suresh embarked on her entrepreneurial journey at age 20, starting with just ₹10,000 gifted by her mother. Despite societal skepticism towards women entrepreneurs 27 years ago, she persevered to build an internationally recognized soft toy manufacturing business.

Through her brands Beazle and TouchMe, she provides high-quality, child-friendly toys certified by the Bureau of Indian Standards. As a member of the Peenya Industries Association and Karnataka Small Scale Industries Association, she advocates for women entrepreneurs and empowers rural communities through skill development.
